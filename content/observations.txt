**EXECUTIVE INSIGHT**

Current evidence suggests MRH is entering a new phase of organisational development, where growth opportunities, strategic partnerships and investor activity are increasing faster than the supporting operational infrastructure. The primary organisational challenge is no longer opportunity generation, but the organisation's capacity to absorb, manage and scale that growth effectively. In this context, successful Odoo implementation remains a critical priority, as it is expected to provide the visibility, accountability and operational discipline required to reduce founder dependency, strengthen governance and support scalable operations across the Group's expanding portfolio and jurisdictions.
